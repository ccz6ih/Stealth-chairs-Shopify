## 1. BRAND POSITIONING: "The Chair Behind the Sound"

### The Core Problem
Every other chair company sells to *everyone*. Herman Miller sells "ergonomic office chairs." Corsair sells "gaming chairs." Even companies that appear on studio chair lists (Sweetwater, etc.) are reselling generic ergonomic furniture with no soul, no story, and no understanding of what it means to sit at FOH for 14 hours during a live show, or mix a record for 6 days straight.

### Stealth's Unfair Advantage
Stealth isn't a chair company that sells to the music industry. **Stealth IS the music industry's chair.** No other seating brand can claim:

- **Patented ZenWave motion technology** — designed specifically for the forward-lean posture of engineers at consoles
- **Free-Float backrest** — follows you as you lean into the mix, not away from it
- **Built in Centennial, Colorado** — not mass-produced overseas
- **Custom-fit to body type** — every chair is built to the buyer's height and weight
- **Real industry credibility** — used by FOH engineers on tours for John Fogerty, Rascal Flatts, The Band Perry, Hunter Hayes, LeAnn Rimes, Timbaland, and more
- **5-year warranty** — one of the longest in the industry

### Brand Positioning Statement
> **Stealth Chairs: Engineered for the people who engineer the sound.**
> From the studio to the stage, from the control room to the broadcast truck — Stealth is the only chair built by people who understand that your next 14-hour session demands more than an office chair with a new label.

## 2. BRAND VOICE & MESSAGING

### Voice Characteristics
- **Authoritative but not corporate** — like a seasoned tour manager, not a furniture salesman
- **Technical but accessible** — speaks the language of engineers without gatekeeping
- **Confident, not boastful** — let the artist roster and tech speak for themselves
- **Community-driven** — "we" and "our industry" not "our product"

### Core Messaging Pillars

#### Pillar 1: "Built for the Session"
The music industry doesn't work 9-to-5. Sessions run 12, 14, 16 hours. Stealth is engineered for the marathon, not the sprint.

**Headlines:**
- "Your mix deserves better than an office chair"
- "14-hour sessions demand 14-hour comfort"
- "Built for the session. Trusted by the engineers."

#### Pillar 2: "Trusted by Legends"
Real credibility from real professionals. Not influencer deals — actual working engineers on actual tours and in actual studios.

**Headlines:**
- "From the console to the stage — trusted by the best"
- "The chair behind your favorite records"
- "When the tour demands the best, engineers choose Stealth"

#### Pillar 3: "Engineered Different"
Patented technology that no other chair has. Not marketing spin — actual mechanical innovation.

**Headlines:**
- "ZenWave technology: your spine's new favorite frequency"
- "The only chair that leans forward with you"
- "Patented. Not replicated."

#### Pillar 4: "Custom to You"
Every body is different. Every Stealth is built to match.

**Headlines:**
- "Your body. Your build. Your Stealth."
- "Not one-size-fits-all. One-size-fits-you."

## 3. VISUAL IDENTITY REFINEMENTS

### Current State Assessment
The current palette is strong — dark charcoal (#1f1f21) with electric lime (#E3FC02) and cyan (#00FCED) accents. This is already more "backstage pass" than "office furniture." However, the implementation feels generic in places.

### Recommended Palette Update

| Role | Current | Recommended | Rationale |
|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Primary Background | #1f1f21 (charcoal) | **Keep** | Dark = studio/backstage vibe |
| Primary Accent | #E3FC02 (lime) | **Keep** | Energy, movement, unique |
| Secondary Accent | #00FCED (cyan) | **Keep but use sparingly** | LED/tech feel, good for data |
| New: Warm Accent | — | **#FF6B35** (deep orange) | Stage lighting warmth, urgency for CTAs |
| New: Muted Gold | — | **#C9A84C** (studio gold) | Premium feel, award/Grammy associations |
| Text Secondary | #ffffff only | **#B8B8B8** (light gray) | Better hierarchy, less harsh |

### Typography Recommendation

| Use | Current | Recommended | Why |
|-----|---------|-------------|-----|
| Headlines | Cabin | **Oswald** (already in preset) | Bolder, more impact, condensed = modern |
| Body | Montserrat | **Keep or switch to Figtree** | Both clean; Figtree is more contemporary |
| Accent/Technical | — | **JetBrains Mono or Source Code Pro** | Console/tech feel for specs, data, badges |

### Design Language Additions
- **Subtle sound-wave patterns** — as dividers, backgrounds, hover states
- **Equalizer-style loading animations** — reinforce music identity
- **Console/fader imagery** — in backgrounds and section transitions
- **Backstage credential aesthetic** — for testimonial cards, badges
- **Spotlight/gradient effects** — mimicking stage lighting for hero sections

## 5. NEW PAGES — PLANNED

### 5A. Industry Landing Pages (Conversion-Focused)

Each page follows the same conversion framework:
**Hero (pain point) → Solution (Stealth features) → Proof (testimonials from that segment) → CTA (shop or quiz)**

#### Page: "For Recording Studios" (`page.for-studios.json`)
- **Hero:** "Your Next Gold Record Starts With How You Sit"
- **Audience:** Recording engineers, mix engineers, mastering engineers, producers
- **Pain points:** 12+ hour mix sessions, forward lean at console, back/neck pain
- **Key features:** ZenWave, Free-Float, console-height adjustment
- **Proof:** Studio photos, engineer testimonials
- **CTA:** "Find Your Stealth" → Quiz or Shop

#### Page: "For Live Sound & FOH" (`page.for-live-sound.json`)
- **Hero:** "From Soundcheck to Encore — Your Chair Should Keep Up"
- **Audience:** FOH engineers, monitor engineers, broadcast audio
- **Pain points:** Festival conditions, uneven surfaces, long show days, transport durability
- **Key features:** Stage Glide casters, portability, durability, custom height
- **Proof:** Tour photos (John Fogerty FOH, Rascal Flatts, etc.)
- **CTA:** "Built for the Road" → Shop with tour bundle

#### Page: "For Video & Broadcast" (`page.for-broadcast.json`)
- **Hero:** "Control Room Comfort That Keeps You Sharp"
- **Audience:** Video engineers, broadcast operators, streaming techs, post-production
- **Pain points:** Multi-monitor setups, 24/7 broadcast shifts, precision work
- **Key features:** Swivel range, height adjustment, ergonomic support during precision tasks
- **Proof:** Broadcast facility photos, church/house of worship installations
- **CTA:** "Upgrade Your Control Room"

#### Page: "For Lighting & Stage" (`page.for-lighting.json`)
- **Hero:** "Design the Show. We'll Support You."
- **Audience:** Lighting designers, stage managers, technical directors
- **Pain points:** Elevated platforms, pre-show programming marathons, tech table setups
- **Key features:** Height options, Stage Glide casters, durability
- **Proof:** Lighting console photos, touring LD testimonials
- **CTA:** "Light It Up" → Shop

#### Page: "For Performers" (`page.for-performers.json`)
- **Hero:** "Play Longer. Sit Better."
- **Audience:** Musicians, drummers (stool alternative), keyboard players, guitar players who sit
- **Pain points:** Traditional stools lack support, posture affects performance
- **Key features:** Performer Series stool, adjustable height, backrest options
- **Proof:** Artist photos, performance settings
- **CTA:** "Find Your Performance Seat"
